What is the Building ontology used for?

The Building ontology is a module that complements the Core ontology with vocabularies for room types and can be extended or modified to suite individual needs.

Specific notes

Class, Properties, and Individuals are all presented in alphabetic order instead of being clustered in their domains or controlled vocabularies.
